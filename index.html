Biography:

Hi! My name is Huazhu Fu. I am a Research Scientist in Institute for Infocomm Research, at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. Earlier, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University, China, in 2013, and worked as a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, for two years.

Research Interests:

  • Saliency detection: Co-saliency, RGBD saliency.
    - A review of "Co-saliency detection" can be found >>>>HERE !
  • Object-based segmentation: Co-segmentation, Video segmentation.
    - The project of "Video co-segmentation" can be found >>>>HERE !
    - The project of "Object-based RGBD segmentation" can be found >>>>HERE !
  • Medical image analysis: Vessel segmentation, OCT/AS-OCT image processing.
    - The project of "DeepVessel: deep learning vessel segmentation" can be found >>>>HERE !
  • Multi-view representation: Multi-view subspace clustering, Multi-View dimensionality reduction.
